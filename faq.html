Bail bond answers from licensed agents

The standard bail bond premium in California is 10% of the total bail amount, regulated by California Insurance Code § 1800.4. For example, if bail is set at $10,000, you pay $1,000. Some clients may qualify for a reduced 8% rate.

Processing time varies by facility, but once we post the bond, most people are released within 4-8 hours. Some facilities are faster, some slower. We expedite the process as much as possible.

LA County operates 7 major facilities: Twin Towers (TTCF) and Men's Central Jail at 450 Bauchet St for men; Century Regional (CRDF) at 11705 Alameda St, Lynwood for women; Pitchess (NCCF/PDC) at 29320 The Old Road, Castaic for minimum-medium security; and the Inmate Reception Center (IRC) for intake. All facilities use the LASD Inmate Locator — call (213) 473-6100.

Most downtown LA arrests are arraigned at the Clara Shortridge Foltz Criminal Justice Center, 210 W Temple St, LA 90012. Other courthouses include Pomona (South), Van Nuys (Valley), Compton (South LA), and Airport (Westside). The specific court depends on arrest location. Call (626) 478-1062 and we'll tell you exactly which court applies.
